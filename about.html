INTRODUCTION | @aditibodhankar

Hello, I am Aditi Bodhankar, a quirky exuberant ambivert from India. I am extremely passionate about photography and art. As a young girl, I always believed that memories can never be the same as time goes by. When a moment is experienced for the first time, the feeling it imparts can never be vividly remembered again. This thought always intrigued me and made me pursue photography as a hobby. With my camera, I wish to cherish all the emotions that I feel in that one particular moment. And through this blog, I wish to share those feelings through every picture captured.

I hope my life stories inspire you to create your own. Have a great time!
